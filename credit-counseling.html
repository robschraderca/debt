What Is Credit Counseling?

One-on-one guidance to help you understand your money—and what to do with it.

Credit counseling is exactly what it sounds like: sitting down (or getting on a call) with someone who knows finances inside and out, and working through your specific situation together.

No generic advice. No judgment. Just a certified professional helping you make sense of your income, expenses, debts, and goals—and creating a realistic plan you can actually follow.

This isn't therapy, but it can feel just as relieving. There's something powerful about finally understanding where your money goes and having a clear plan for what to do next.

Who It's For

Credit counseling isn't just for people in crisis.

Whether you're drowning or just want to swim better, having a guide makes the journey easier.

What to Expect

Your first session—no surprises.

  • No judgment, ever

    Your counselor has heard it all. There's nothing you can say that will shock them or make them think less of you.

  • Everything stays confidential

    What you share in session stays in session. We're not reporting to anyone or sharing your information.

  • Questions are encouraged

    There are no dumb questions. If something doesn't make sense, ask. That's what we're here for.
