What Is a Personal Loan?

Flexible funding without putting your assets at risk

A personal loan is money you borrow based on your creditworthiness — no home equity, no car title, no collateral. You receive a lump sum, repay it over a fixed term with fixed payments, and use it for almost anything.

No Collateral

Your home and car stay yours. Approval is based on credit, not assets.

Fixed Payments

Same amount, same date, every month. No surprises.

Fast Funding

Many lenders fund within a few days of approval.

Flexible Use

Use it for almost any purpose — you decide.

Is This Right For You?

We'll be honest about whether this fits your situation

A personal loan might be right if...

  • You need funds for a specific purpose
  • You want predictable monthly payments
  • Your credit is fair to excellent
  • You can comfortably afford the payments
  • You don't want to risk collateral

Consider other options if...

  • You're already struggling with payments
  • You're dealing with debt collectors (try Financial Restoration)
  • You need to simplify existing debt (try Debt Consolidation)
  • Adding more debt would make things worse
  • You need financial guidance first (try Credit Counseling)
